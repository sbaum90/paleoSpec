The neutrino fluxes are taken from Ciaran O'Hares code for the 
CYGNUS paper https://arxiv.org/abs/2008.12587, taken from
his GitHub repository
https://github.com/cajohare/CYGNUS
on Oct 19, 2020

The CAJOH_*_shape.dat files contain the spectral shapes, but are
normalized to 1.
The CAJOH_*.dat files have the correct normalization of the
respective spectra.
